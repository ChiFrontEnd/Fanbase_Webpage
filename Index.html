Biography

Formed under BIGHIT MUSIC, CORTIS was created with a vision of blending artistic innovation, storytelling, and performance. The group rapidly gained international attention through their debut project and became known for their powerful stage presence and unique concept development. With multiple music-show victories, global fan engagement, and millions of streams, CORTIS continues to expand its influence worldwide.
